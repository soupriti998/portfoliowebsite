## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and product leaders at AI/SaaS companies (Indian tech ecosystem + global remote). They visit during deliberate job searches or networking. Job to be done: quickly assess design maturity, taste, and fit in under 5 minutes. They need to feel confident about Soupriti's ability to own product design end-to-end.

### Brand Personality
Warm · Intelligent · Unhurried

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Warm neutral palette (OKLCH, terracotta clay accent at oklch(58% 0.12 42)). Editorial typography: Bricolage Grotesque (display) + Libre Baskerville (italic accents) + Geist (body). Anti-references: dark sci-fi, neon, Dribbble-style maximalism, cluttered motion. Inspirations: Airbnb, Linear, Notion, Apple editorial pages.

### Design Principles
1. **Spaciousness signals confidence** — generous whitespace is the message, not a gap to fill
2. **Typography carries emotion** — the serif italic is the warmth layer; Bricolage is the clarity layer
3. **Motion is breath, not performance** — fade-ups, no bounces, no particles
4. **Asymmetry feels considered** — sticky left column, flowing right; magazine rhythm
5. **Accents are rare** — terracotta clay is precious; use it for one thing at a time
